Niko - May to June 2023

This portfolio site is in dedication to my foster kitten. I fostered Niko for only a short month, but I will always remember his energy and spirit. He was a spunky boy that loved to play and was an exceptionally brave little guy.

Sadly, I noticed he was having issues going to the bathroom and took him to the vet to get it checked out. The vet confirmed he had a rare genetic deformity called megacolon, which causes a colon to grow up to four times larger than a normal colon. This causes lifelong issues and struggles and the vet said the best option was to euthanize as it was difficult to treat and surgery would not improve much. So, to Niko, I dedicate this portfolio site. You climbed on my shoulder during my coding schooling and distracted everyone in class with your cuteness. May you always chase all the butterflies and receive all the love in kitty heaven.
